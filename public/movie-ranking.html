The Best Movie According to me

My top 3 movie of all time

Oldboy (2003) :

A man is inexplicably imprisoned for 15 years, only to be released and given five days to find his captor.

Memories of Murder (2003) :

Based on a true story, two detectives struggle to catch a serial killer terrorizing a small town in South Korea.

The Wailing (2016) :

A mysterious illness and a string of brutal murders plague a rural village, leading a police officer into a dark and terrifying investigation.
